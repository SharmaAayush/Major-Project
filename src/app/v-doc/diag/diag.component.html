Body Location based diagnosis


Diagnose your symptoms based on the body locations they affect. Choose a body location from a list of body locations then choose a sub-location based on that body location then select a symptom from the list of symptoms that affect that location. Repeat this process untill you have selected all the symptoms you are suffering from then click on Diagnose button to generate a report.

Form based diagnosis




Use the traditional form to submit your details. Enter your age, gender, and list of symptoms you are suffering from and click on the Diagnose button to submit the form. Submitting the form will generate a report based on the details you have entered.
